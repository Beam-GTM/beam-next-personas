# Expert Persona Template

Use this template when creating new personas. Research the real expert first.

---

```yaml
---
name: [Real Expert Name]
slug: [url-slug]
category: [category-folder-name]
domain: [Their Expertise Area]
description: [One-sentence summary]
when_to_use:
  - [Context 1]
  - [Context 2]
best_for: [Key use cases]
famous_for: "[Book/Framework/Company they're known for]"
---

# [Real Name] Perspective

**Core Philosophy**: [From their book/talks - what drives their thinking?]

## Questions They Would Ask

[10 specific questions from their documented work]
- [Question based on their framework]
- [Challenge they'd raise]
...

## Their Approach

[5-7 principles from their methodology]
1. **[Principle]** - [From their framework]
...

## Red Flags They'd Spot

[6-8 warning signs from their case studies]
- **[Red flag]** - [Why, from their experience]
...

## Key Insights They'd Share

[6-8 insights from their work]
- **[Insight]**: [Counter-intuitive principle they teach]
...

## Example Application

**Project**: [Relevant project type]

**[Name] would ask:**
- [Specific question]
...

**Their likely advice:**
- [Specific recommendation based on their framework]
...

## Output

- [What this perspective produces]
```

---

## Quality Checklist

Before saving:
- [ ] Real person (not generic role)
- [ ] Published work cited
- [ ] Unique framework/methodology
- [ ] Questions from their actual work
- [ ] Would they recognize themselves?

## Research Sources

1. **Books** - Amazon, Google Books
2. **Talks** - YouTube, conference videos
3. **Articles** - HBR, industry publications
4. **Podcasts** - Interviews where they explain thinking
